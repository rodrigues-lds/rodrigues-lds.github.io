The Purpose of this Portal

The Weather Seer is a webpage service created by Eduardo Almeida Rodrigues for Brigham Young University, class WDD230. The weather forecast service portal will be published at www.theweatherseer.com available and checked its availability at www.hostgator.com. It provides a good weather forecast service for anyone who is interested in knowing what will happen to the weather in the coming hours and days. It aims to be a main portal to help people plan their work, events and so on, without being affected by the climatic conditions of their regions, specially for people from Preston, Soda Springs, and Fish Haven. For education purposes, this website consumes services and contents provided and licensed by OpenWeatherMap and Deseret News. People who wants to plan activities and events outsite of their houses as well as those professionals who depends on good weather to perform their work are welcome. Additionally, anyone interested in checking the weather conditions in the mentioned cities.
