Target Audience

People who wants to plan activities and events outsite of their houses as well as those professionals who depends on good weather to perform their work. Additionally, anyone interested in checking the weather conditions.

Personas

John Mario

John Mario is a member of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. He is 45 years old and loves to walk, ski and play with his kids outside the home and in places like mountains and parks. He loves to plan future events and, depending on the weather, he can plan to go to the mountains to snowboard or play football on sunny days with his family. He believes that these activities with his family will reduce the daily stress. As an engineer, he finds in this activities a great chance to be closer to his family.

Mary May

Mary May is a manager at a car wash company. She has worked as a manager for 10 years. On sunny days she uses water to wash the cars of the company's customers. On the other hand, if it is raining, she uses a technique called dry cleaning, in which no water is needed to clean the car. However, the latter option is more expensive for her and her customers. It depends on the weather to determine which technique she will use on a daily basis.

Scenarios

  • Will the mountains be coverd with snow?
  • What will be the weather conditions this weekend?
  • Do I have to bring an umbrella and a coat with me tomorrow?
  • Will tomorrow be a good day for a barbecue with my friends?
  • Is that ok to invite my friends for a party in the backyard saturday night?
