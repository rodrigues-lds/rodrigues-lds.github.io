The Weather Seer

A weather forecast service portal that will be published at www.theweatherseer.com available and checked at www.hostgator.com.

Weather Site Purpose

Provide a good weather forecast service for anyone who is interested in knowing what will happen to the weather in the coming hours and days. It aims to be a main portal to help people plan their work, events and so on, without being affected by the climatic conditions of their regions.
