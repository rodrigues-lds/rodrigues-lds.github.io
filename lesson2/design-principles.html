Visual Hierarchy Principle

CNN NEWS

www.cnn.com

CNN News page use visual hierarchy by applying size and scale on images and fonts to pull focus. They give more importance to specific news than others in order to draw the reader's eye toward the main news of the moment.

PARC: Alignment Principle

Leroy Merlin

www.leroymerlin.com.br

Leroy Merlin web site provides a great alignment of its contents and blocks. Their sections are aligned, organizing them and helping the user to select and find what they want. It helps the reader to find where elements are placed and how they interact.

White Space & Clean Design

Apple

www.apple.com

White space is the area between design elements. A well-made white space design improves readability and performance of your website. There is a great white space between the sections in apple website, specially when the content or subject is changed. This is visible on One and Card section.
